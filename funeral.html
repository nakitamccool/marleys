Services

We take care and consideration when understanding, planning and providing services to commemorate and celebrate the life of a loved one.

Our team of Funeral Directors are experienced in:

  • Planning a personalised funeral service
  • Providing advice on flowers, music and other requirements for the service
  • Liaising with the celebrant
  • Providing transport (for the deceased and bereaved family)
  • Arranging death notices
  • Arranging repatriation (to and from Ireland)
  • Assisting you in organising a death certificate
  • Preparing the body of the deceased in a dignified and hygienic manner
  • Advising and organising coffins or caskets
  • Arranging burial or cremation
  • Arranging grave opening, dressing and closing services
  • Providing after funeral care, as requested

Repatriation

Returning home

If a loved one has passed away abroad, we can help in getting your loved one home. We have specialist expertise in arranging the repatriation both to and from Ireland.

Our team handles all the logistics of the repatriation process, liaising with coroners, embassies and airlines so that we can bring your loved one home in a dignified manner.

To find out more or to arrange a repatriation, please contact us and we'll be happy to help.

Our History

Joe Francie Marley founded the funeral service in 1978 in the parish of Glenfin, Co. Donegal. At this time he was in the car, bus & coach hire business. The local Funeral Director Dan Mc Menamin who ran a very successful funeral service was due to retire.

Joe was committed to assisting sensitively those coping with bereavement. He provided support with a full range of professional funeral arrangement services. Joe then retired himself in 2010 and the business was continued by the Marley family.
